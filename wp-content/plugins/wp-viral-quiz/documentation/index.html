Install

1. Go on your Wordpress Admin

 

2. Click "Plugins » Add new"

 

3. Click "Upload plugin"

 

4. Select the plugin zipped file and validate

 

5. Activate the plugin

Create a TrueFalse Quiz

1. Open WP Viral Quiz

 

2. Chose "Create a True/False Quiz"

 

3. Enter the name of your quiz

 

4. Create your first question 

5. Save the Quiz

 

6. Back on the WP Viral Quiz dashboard where your quiz is now present

 

7.  To insert a quiz in your article or page : copy and paste the shortcode to the content of your page/post, and save it ! The quiz will appear on the page.

Enable Facebook Share Button

To activate the Facebook Share Button on WP Viral Quiz, you have to create your own Facebook APP. Don’t panic, it’s very easy ! It take 2 minutes.

 

1. Go on your Personnal Facebook Account.

Yup, first step is easy. Go on www.facebook.com and log in to your personal account.

 

2. Go on the Facebook Developper Website

Just click here to go on the Facebook Dev Site : https://developers.facebook.com/apps/

 

3. Click on “Create a new app”

 

4. Choose “Website”

5. Click on “Skip and Create App ID”

6. Type a name, set a category (“communication” is OK, we don’t care) and validate.

 

7. Go on the Settings Page of your Facebook App

8. Click on “Add Plateform”, type your website URL in the field “Site URL” and save changes.

 

9. Copy and paste your “APP ID” on the WP Viral Quiz settings page (on your blog !)

 

 

Save changes, et voila ! It’s finished. People can share their results when they play your quizzes.
